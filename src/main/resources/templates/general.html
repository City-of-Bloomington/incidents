To add an incident you will go through the following steps:

  1. Add incident details (next page)
  2. Add persons' info including witnesses (if any)
  3. Add properties related to incident
  4. Add vehicle info if the incident include a vehicle
  5. Upload incident image(s) (if any)
  6. Submit your request
  7. You must confirm your request via the email address you provide.

After a successful submission, our staff will evaluate your incident and either approve or reject your request. You will receive an email about the staff descision. If approved, the email will include a case number that you can use for future reference to your incident.

After review, if additional follow-up is needed, you will be contacted by a member of the Bloomington Police Department Detective Division within 3-5 business days.

Please complete the fields below. The information you provide for your case is critical to the Department's overall crime analysis efforts.

  • Information about the Person Reporting this Incident
  • Please add information about the person reporting this incident. If you are the complainant and victim, please only enter your information once, choosing either complainant or victim as the involvement type.
  • After completing your information, you will have the option to add additional people to the report, such as property owners, other victims and witnesses.

Use this page to report property that was lost, stolen or damaged.

  • You are able to add multiple pieces of property, however, you can only add one piece of property at a time
  • If you are reporting damage to a vehicle, please give the description and value of the damage in the vehicle screen.

This form is REQUIRED if a vehicle was entered, vandalized, damaged, or in any way involved in this incident.

  • You may add one or more vehicles
  • You can one vehicle at a time
  • Please report vehicle damage including value in the vehicle screen.
  • You can upload up to 5 images related to incident.
  • Optional, you may add explantions about each image in the text field below.
  • The accepted image files are jpeg, gif, png and pdf only any other type will be ignored
  • Images size more than 2 MG will be rejected
  • If image file size is more than 2 MB, you need to resize it using one of the image editing tools such as MS paint
  • When done with image uploads, click on 'Submit'
  • You can upload one file at a time
