Welcome to Incident Reporting

Guidelines for Using the Bloomington Police Department Online Reporting System.

Note: Filing a false police report is a crime and is punishable by up to 180 days in jail and a fine of up to $1000.

You may report one of the following incident types:

  1. An individual incident: that may include one of the following incident categories:
    • Lost Property
    • Theft From Vehicle
    • Theft From Building
    • Theft (All Other)
    • Fraud & Scam
    • Vandalism
  2. File business incident. The incident could be one of the following categories:
    • Theft
    • Vandalism
