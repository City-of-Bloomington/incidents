Incident Reporting for Business

To add an incident you would go through a number of steps depending on the incident type. The following information are needed to complete an incident.

  • Business Address
  • Business Info
  • Select the incident type (see below)
  • Incident date, time and details
  • Offender(s) involved in the incident
  • Damaged properties
  • Upload photo of the receipts for theft or photo(s) for vandalism
  • Submit the incident
  • Our staff will check the incident, they may approve or reject the incident
  • The business will be informed by email of the staff descision
  • Please gather the needed information before you continue

Please, select the incident category:

  • Theft
  • Vandalism
