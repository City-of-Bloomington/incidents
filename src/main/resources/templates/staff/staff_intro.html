You can perfom one of the following options depending on your privileges.

  • Received: Handle newly recieved incidents
  • Confirmed: Handle newly email confirmed incidents
  • Approved/Rejected: Handle recently approved/rejected incidents
  • Search: Search all incidents by incident number, type, date, etc
  • Reports: Run stats reports for certain period of time, week, month, year, or date range
  • Setting: Limited to 'Admin' group to handle app different types of settings
